Farmer-Buyer Platform – User Guide

For Farmers & Buyers

Login Farmer Features Buyer Features Payment & Orders Profile Tips & FAQs

Logging In

  • Open the app and enter your mobile number.
  • Enter the OTP received via SMS to login.

After entering your mobile number, an OTP will be sent. Enter it to verify and access your account.

Farmer Features

  • Add Product: Fill in name, price, quantity, location → Save.
  • Edit/Delete Product: Go to "My Products" and select a product.
  • View All Products: Browse the store and use search/filters.
  • Track Orders: See buyer info, product, quantity, total price, and status.
  • Update Order Status: Mark as processing, completed, or cancelled.

Farmers can manage their products and orders efficiently. The platform updates buyers automatically when the status changes.

Buyer Features

  • Browse Products: Use search and filters in the store.
  • Show Interest: Click "Interested" on a product and contact the farmer.
  • Track Orders: Check order list with status: pending, paid, processing, completed, cancelled.

Buyers can easily browse products and express interest. Order tracking keeps them informed about payment and delivery.

Payment & Order Tracking

  • Make Payment: Select product → Pay Now → Enter card details → Complete payment.
  • Payment Status: Automatically updated in the order list for both buyer and farmer.
  • Order Status: Updated by farmer after processing or completing the order.

Payments are secure and automatically reflected in the system. Both parties are notified about order progress in real-time.

Profile Management

  • Update name and mobile number from "Profile".
  • Switch between Buyer/Farmer mode.

Tips & FAQs

  • Payment fails → Retry or check internet connection.
  • Farmer hasn’t updated status → Refresh order page.
  • All paid orders appear as "Paid" for both buyer and farmer.
